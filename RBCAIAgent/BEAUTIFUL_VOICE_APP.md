## 🎯 **How to Use - Super Simple**

### **Voice Chat Mode:**
1. **Tap the microphone button** 🎤 in chat
2. **Beautiful full-screen voice interface** appears
3. **Tap "Start Talking"** - watch the circle glow red
4. **Speak naturally** - see your words appear
5. **AI responds** - circle turns green while speaking
6. **Auto-returns to chat** after response

### **Regular Chat Mode:**
1. **Type your question** in the modern input field
2. **Tap send** or use voice for hands-free
3. **Beautiful message bubbles** appear with animations
4. **AI responds** with formatted text

### **Quick Voice Tips:**
- **"What's my balance?"** - Instant balance info
- **"Recent transactions"** - See spending
- **"Help"** - Get assistance
