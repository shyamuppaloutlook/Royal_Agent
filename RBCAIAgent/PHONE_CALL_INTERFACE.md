# 📞 Beautiful Phone Call Interface for RBC AI Assistant

## 🎨 Premium Call Experience

Instead of just voice chat, you now have a **beautiful phone call interface** that feels like calling your personal banking assistant!

### 📱 **How It Works:**

1. **Tap the green phone button** 📞 in chat
2. **Beautiful call screen appears** with RBC branding
3. **AI answers naturally**: "Hello! Thanks for calling RBC Assistant. How can I help you today?"
4. **Have a real conversation** - just like talking to a person
5. **See live transcription** of what you're saying
6. **End call anytime** with the red button
